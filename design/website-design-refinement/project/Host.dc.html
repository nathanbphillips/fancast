FROM ACCOUNT TO ON AIR

Six steps, about a minute of setup.

01
Become a commentator

Upgrade from settings: read the terms, tick the box, host today. No application, no waiting.

02
Create a room

Pick a fixture, set your start time and a one-line blurb. Not listed? Spin one up for any game. Every room gets a clean, shareable link.

03
Host a whole season

One click schedules a room for every game your team plays. New fixtures get theirs automatically.

04
Bring a co-host

Invite another supporter as an equal. Either of you runs everything; both of you keep the recordings.

05
Go on air

Open the waiting room, start the broadcast, drive the clock, moderate the chat, take call-ins and push stats.

06
Keep your show

Every broadcast is recorded and cut into downloadable segments. One hundred percent yours.

THE ONE RULE

Audio only, always.

Your listeners bring their own screens; you bring the commentary. Never play match video or broadcast audio through your show, even in the background. It's the one thing we can't bend, and it's what keeps the whole platform on the right side of the line.

QUICK ANSWERS

The bits hosts ask

What gear do I need?

A microphone and a browser. A quiet room and a decent headset beat expensive kit.

Do my listeners pay?

No. Listening is free and needs no account; people sign up only to chat, vote or call in.

What if my match has no stats?

Chat, audio and recordings all work regardless. Uncovered games just show “coming soon” for stats while we add more leagues.

Something broke mid-show?

Keep talking. Listeners reconnect automatically and the recording runs server-side, so a glitch on your end never loses the show.
